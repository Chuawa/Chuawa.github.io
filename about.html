CHUA WANG AN

Born in Singapore in 1992. An introverted person with the ability to be extraordinarily extroverted when needed. Enjoy reading wide ranging topics of literatures and stuffs, particularly current affairs and politics (Which is rather boring for friends my age but not me!). Talk to me on politics and current affairs that are happening around the world and I will be able to keep you extremely engaged in the conversation.

I work hard and enjoy excelling at work that I truly enjoy. Doing my best in all that I have decided to do is my personal ethos. I believe in helping others when I have the extra capacity and resource to do it. I care about my friends and believe in full honesty. I demand honesty from people around me and I will treat them with full honesty too. I am a heavy day-dreamer with a perceived "ideal" world that we should be living in. Always critical about the way and the world we live in, questioning non-stop about all the rules and norms. Always hungry and driven to better myself and continue to learn extensively.

Self confessed technology enthusiast (especially computer programming and electronics). Because I belief that back in the past, we need to be popular and capable politicians to be able to change the world, however, with the proper skillsets of technology in today's era, any average Joe will have his opportunity to change the world without the need to be politician or some other power struggling-needed positions. I dream of one day to be able to change the world with my own invention and creation.

I subscribe to the belief of Christianity from being a long time free-thinker. However, continuing to find alot of contradictory areas, and am continuing to seek understanding of those areas.
