SNEC Hand Hygiene tracking System

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Development of an overall system with sensors capable of detecting the patient presence in front of the eye machine and able to perform transmitting, receiving, storing and displaying of data wirelessly to the nearest computer terminal. It will be able to operate at the maximum rate possible from the maximum number of devices allowed.

The system should be able to perform:

 Alerting the operator/doctor to sanitize their hands upon the detection of a patient and to record comply/non-comply behavior.

 Alerting the operator/doctor to sanitize their hands after the patient had left the eye machine and to record comply/non-comply behavior.

 Storing of data for analysis.

The system will be used to improve the accuracy of tracking compliance rate of hand hygiene in the clinic.
